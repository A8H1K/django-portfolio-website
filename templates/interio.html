About Us

Video Intro

We “Interio Lighting” situated at Ahmedabad (Gujarat, India) established in the year 2012, have gained accreditation in the field of manufacturing customized lights , trading and supplying an outstanding range of Indoor Lighting and Outdoor Lighting.

Our firm is supported by advanced infrastructural base which is constructed over a large area of land. To manage our infrastructural base in a smooth manner, we have a team of competent Professionals who are expert in their work domain.

Owing to the support and assistance of our team and our advanced infrastructural base, we are able to serve the voluminous market demands. In order to achieve the company goals and objectives, our team members out forth their maximum efforts.

  • Customized Lights
  • Creative work
  • All India delivery
  • 24x7 support
